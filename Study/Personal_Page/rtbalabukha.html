About Me

A small introduction

I'm José Ramón,

a Panamanian Engineer, Developer, Inventor, Designer, and Musician.

Some of my areas of expertise include software development (primarily in C and Python), web design (mostly front-end, and a bit of back-end), and electronics design. Thinking in a logical and clear way has helped me during my academic and professional career, by being a problem-solver and someone who can be relied on. My friends describe me as honest, responsible, hard worker, and a good listener. If not spending my time working or studying, you will probably find me playing the bass guitar, playing board games or maybe a bit of chess, listening to music, reading Quora or a good book, exploring or just wandering outside, or simply just having a good time with my friends. I believe that we only live once, and we must give our 100%, outdo ourselves, and always be up for new challenges.
